# SenseGNAT Branding Guide

## Brand intent

SenseGNAT should feel observant, analytical, calm, and predictive. It is not an aggressive superhero variant. It is the GNAT-o-sphere product that sees the pattern before the operator does.

## Color system

### Primary
- Deep Violet `#5B2DA8`
- Signal Purple `#7E57FF`

### Supporting neutrals
- Dark Ink `#111321`
- Graphite `#2B2F3C`
- Mist `#ECEAF8`
- Cloud `#F6F5FB`

### Optional accent
- Signal Cyan `#67D7F7`

## Mascot guidance

Keep these constant across GNAT product variants:

- same face shape and eye style
- same body silhouette
- visible wings
- chest badge or shield
- approachable cartoon energy

Adapt these for SenseGNAT:

- purple suit instead of red-forward heroic treatment
- fingers at the temples or a focused "processing" pose
- subtle network arcs, signal ripples, or halo-like data waves
- no exaggerated combat pose
- no mystical or wizard-like cues

## Emblem guidance

Use a shield that contains either:

- a three-arc signal motif
- a node-and-link signal path
- a waveform over a protected boundary line

Preferred choice: **shield with signal arcs**

## Typography guidance

Use bold, condensed display typography for the wordmark and straightforward technical typography in documentation. The product should look operational, not whimsical, even though the mascot remains friendly.

## Tagline

**Behavior is the signal.**
